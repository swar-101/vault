
**SCL 1 — “I get the vibe.”**  
You’re starting to understand the idea. You can maybe follow along if someone else explains it slowly. Still foggy, but not lost.  
👉 Example: “I sorta get recursion — like, it’s a function calling itself, right?”

**SCL 2 — “I can explain it, but not confidently.”**  
You understand the concept and can use it, but there are gaps. You might hesitate, second-guess, or struggle with edge cases.  
👉 Example: “I can implement a linked list, but if you ask about circular ones, I’ll need Google.”

**SCL 3 — “I could teach this.”**  
You’ve mastered it. You can explain, apply, and even improvise around the concept with clarity. You don’t just _know_ it — you _own_ it.  
👉 Example: “I can teach recursion with fun analogies and solve problems live.”

In short:  
**0 = Lost**  
**1 = Learning**  
**2 = Understand with gaps**  
**3 = Teach with confidence**
