# Pattern: Greedy Bit Construction 

## Recognition 

Goal: 
Maximize a binary value. 

Question: 
*Can I decide each bit independently from MSB → LSB?* 

## Invariant 

Every accepted bit is feasible. 

## Why It Works? 

A more significant bit contributes more than all lower bits combined.
