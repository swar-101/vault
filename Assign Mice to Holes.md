**Problem Description**

N Mice and N holes are placed in a straight line. Each hole can accommodate only one mouse. 
The positions of Mice are denoted by array A, and the positions of holes are denoted by array B. 

A mouse can stay at his position, move one right from `x` to `x + 1`, or move one step left from `x` to `x - 1`. Any of these moves consume 1 minute.

Assign mice to holes so that the time when the last mouse gets inside a hole is minimized. 

--- 

**Problem Constraints**
1 <= N <= 10<sup>5</sup>
-10<sup>9</sup> <= A\[i\], B\[i\] <= 10<sup>9</sup>
